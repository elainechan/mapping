Bike Me

About this map

- Features: use the dark button set on the top right to show or hide features.

- Declutter: click the 'hide' button on the top right to hide menus and logos. Click 'show' to display them.

- Bike parking and public benches: click on a symbol to see more information.

- Map style: use the top menu to switch map style.

This map is made with Mapbox and NYC Open Data.
